Research & Publications

AnantaNetra: A Hybrid AI-Powered Environmental Monitoring and Health Advisory System for India's Air Quality Crisis

Journal: Journal of Emerging Technologies and Innovative Research (JETIR) | Published: September 16, 2025 | Impact Factor: 7.95 | UGC-Approved, Peer-Reviewed International Journal

Published research presenting "AnantaNetra," a novel hybrid AI framework designed to address India's critical air quality crisis. The research involved the development and deployment of a custom-built LSTM+XGBoost ensemble model, achieving 92% accuracy for real-time Air Quality Index (AQI) monitoring and 24-hour forecasting across all 732 districts in India. A significant undertaking was the creation of a proprietary, unified dataset, meticulously curated from over 15 distinct environmental and meteorological data sources to ensure comprehensive spatial and temporal coverage.

The implemented system provides actionable, AI-powered health advisories and supports evidence-based policy making, directly aligning with the objectives of India's National Clean Air Programme (NCAP). The scalable microservices architecture ensures robustness and reliability, demonstrating the practical application of machine learning for large-scale environmental monitoring and public health protection.
